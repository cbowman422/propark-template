About Empire

Established in 1968, Empire, a privately-owned transportation company, evolved from a parking management firm. Propark acquired Empire in 2023, reinforcing our commitment to excellence.

Today, Empire leads the transportation industry, serving diverse clients with unwavering customer dedication. Our legacy has instilled a culture of exceptional service from leadership to drivers, earning Empire the highest customer service reputation in Southern California.
